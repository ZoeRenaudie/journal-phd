abstract: On October and November 2024, I delivered a series of lectures in Athens, Amsterdam and Barcelona on the topic of “The end of conservation”. After these lectures, I also developed a research paper on this same topic. In all versions of "The end of conservation" I explore some ‘symptoms that may be discerned in the cloud of beliefs, ideas, terms, concepts and practices that nowadays make up the field of cultural heritage conservation. If seen in combination, these symptoms may suggest that an extraordinary, radical change could be taking place in the field of cultural heritage conservation.
